HERE'S THE THING...

Online shopping could be better... MUCH better.

Are you tired of searching for the needle in the haystack?

Us too. So we made this site. And bring the needle to you.

Yay for us, am I right?

For reals, though...

During the pandemic lockdown "fun" of 2020, we found ourselves shopping online... a lot.

I was searching for some workout equipment after the gyms got shut down a second time. But Facebook Marketplace, letgo, OfferUp, good ole craigslist and other sites were riddled with miscategorized items and scammy sellers.

Unless you're buying things brand new on amazon, finding used stuff is damn near impossible.

I guess I should really be grateful. I was spending a lot of time navigating the different sites, sending off a ton of dead-end messages, getting snaked by other buyers and generally ending up frustrated, and without the stuff I was looking for.

It was then that I had the idea to let the sellers come to me. Similar to a matchmaking service, our aim is to let the buyer control the process. Spin this thing on its head, right?

You make the listing for what you want,
then sit back and let the sellers find you,
and compete for your business.

Bing. Bang. Boom!
